Justicia suspende artículos de la Reforma Laboral
A federal court in Argentina has suspended two articles of the labor reform law, approved in late February, following a request from the Federación Argentina de Empleados de Comercio y Servicios (Faecys). The court's measure, effective for six months, blocks articles 131 and 133 of the reform, which limited the ultraactivity of labor agreements and established new limits on union contributions. The suspension aims to protect the conditions of labor agreements and the benefits of employees in the commerce sector.

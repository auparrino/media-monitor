Artemis II a la luna
The NASA's Artemis II mission successfully flew by the dark side of the Moon, reaching a record distance of 406,778 kilometers from Earth, surpassing the previous record set by the Apollo 13 mission in 1970. The four astronauts on board, Reid Wiseman, Victor Glover, Christina Koch, and Jeremy Hansen, spent 57 minutes in complete darkness, observing and photographing the solar corona during their passage through the Moon's shadow. The mission, which began on a historic note, will continue for another nine days, with the crew capturing unprecedented images of the Moon's hidden side.
